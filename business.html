Engaje Business

Engaje business is a mobile/ web application where businesses can transact with their customers, track visitors and send notifications. The business owners get e-commerce or a booking application. They can add or edit products, change prices, track receipts from customers. Generally, Engaje works as an automatic partner to Amazon, UberEats, and other aggregating businesses. The idea is to gain customers via the aggregators and then own them by converting their orders to Engaje.
