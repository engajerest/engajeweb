What is Community Commerce?

Community commerce is doing business with the people you share a common interest with.

People organise themselves into communities and interact with a community in some way or the other. E.g., Religious communities, Ethnic Groups, Sporting Clubs, Business Associations, Network Groups. Engaje helps those people who are part of that community to give back to the community supporters and sponsors by creating a transactional platform where people can do business with those supporters that they trust and share a common interest. Engaje communities make it easy to see the supporters and sponsors to your communities. See all the takeaways you love, see the local shops in your local suburb, see the sponsors of your sporting clubs and other groups you belong to.

The Engaje Community

Any social group, sporting body or a home owners group (strata) can register themselves to be a community in Engaje. The head of the group (also the Community Owner) lists all their sponsors/supporters/local businesses in the Community and connects their web shops or booking systems to Engaje.

The community owner (host) then encourages all members in the community to download the Engaje application and make their online purchases through the application directly.
